How to Mix Saw Gas: The proper way

Description

For the sake of simplicity let's assume 5 gallons of saw mix is being made at a 50:1 ratio.

Items Required:

  • 5 Gallon Jerry
  • Stihl HP Ultra (makes 5 gallons)

Process

  1. Fill Jerry can approximately half full with ethanol free gasoline.
  2. Empty HP Ultra bottle into Jerry can.
  3. Squirt a little gas back into HP Ultra bottle and shake to remove remaining mix from bottle walls, pour remaining mix/gas into Jerry.
  4. Continue filling the Jerry with gasoline until full.
  5. Give Jerry several shakes to properly mix gas and oil.

Congrats! You now have a true 50:1 2 stroke mix.
